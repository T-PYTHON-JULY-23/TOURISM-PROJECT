Welcome to Saudi Arabia the kingdom of goodwill.

RIYADH CITY

Step back in time as you explore the historical sites of Riyadh. Visit the majestic Masmak Fortress and delve into its fascinating past. Wander through the bustling streets of Diriyah, the UNESCO World Heritage Site, and witness the preserved beauty of traditional mud-brick architecture.

MEKKAH CITY

But Makkah Al-Mukarramah is not just a Kaaba and a sanctuary, it is also a city that embraces visitors with its open arms. Jabal Al-Noor, which rises high in the city skyline, attracts visitors seeking meditation and spirituality. Visitors climb to its summit, breathe the fresh air and enjoy the majestic mountain scenery.
