Explore Jeddah

Where Tradition Meets Modernity, Unveiling the Riches of Saudi Arabia's Coastal Gem!

Jeddah is known as the "Bride of the Red Sea" due to its stunning waterfront and vibrant atmosphere. The city has a population of over 3.4 million people, making it the second-largest city in Saudi Arabia after Riyadh. Jeddah is a melting pot of cultures, with residents from various backgrounds, including Saudi nationals, expatriates, and pilgrims visiting for religious purposes.
