Unveiling the Enchanting Beauty of Saudi Arabia's Hidden Gem!

One of the main attractions in Abha is its stunning mountainous surroundings. The city is nestled in the Sarawat Mountains, which provide breathtaking views and opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking and camping. The mountains are also home to several picturesque villages that offer a glimpse into traditional Saudi Arabian life, Abha is also known for its vibrant festivals and events. The city hosts the annual Abha Festival, which attracts artists, musicians, and performers from around the world. During this festival, the city comes alive with cultural performances, art exhibitions, and traditional food stalls.
