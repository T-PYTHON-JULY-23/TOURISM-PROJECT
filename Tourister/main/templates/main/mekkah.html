Explore Mekkah

Experience the Spiritual Journey of a Lifetime!

The city of Makkah has undergone significant development over the years to accommodate the increasing number of pilgrims. The Masjid al-Haram, which surrounds the Kaaba, has been expanded multiple times to accommodate millions of worshippers during peak times. The Abraj Al-Bait Clock Tower, one of the tallest buildings in the world, is also located in Makkah and serves as a prominent landmark. Every year, millions of Muslims from all over the world undertake a pilgrimage called Hajj to Makkah. Hajj is one of the Five Pillars of Islam and is obligatory for all able-bodied Muslims who can afford it.
