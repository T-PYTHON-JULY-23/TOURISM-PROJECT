Makkah City

Makkah Al-Mukarramah is one of the most important cities in Saudi Arabia. It is considered a sacred place for Muslims around the world, where they perform the Hajj and Umrah pilgrimage. Makkah is home to prominent religious landmarks such as the Kaaba and the Masjid al-Haram. Visiting Makkah is a unique spiritual experience for Muslims and an opportunity to connect with Muslims from all over the world.
