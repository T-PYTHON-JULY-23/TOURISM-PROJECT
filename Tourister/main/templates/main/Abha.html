Abha

The city of Abha is a city high on the top of the Hijaz mountain range, and the city of Abha is very rainy. Its climate is moderately cold in summer, while it is snowy and very cold in winter. The climate of Abha may reach a maximum of 25 ° C in the summer and may reach -4 ° and -6 ° in the winter.
