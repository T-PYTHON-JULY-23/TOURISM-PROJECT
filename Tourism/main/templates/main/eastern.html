Saudi Regions: The Eastern Province

The Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia extends from the shallow coasts of the Persian Gulf through the palm-tree-fringed hills of Al-Ahsa Oasis to the depths of the Empty Quarter desert. Historically, it enjoyed its position as a meeting place for great civilizations such as the Levant, Mesopotamia and India, which made it a thriving commercial center.
