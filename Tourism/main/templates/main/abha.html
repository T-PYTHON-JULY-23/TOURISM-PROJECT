Abha, the scenic capital of Asir Province, has offered some amazing natural wonders that you are bound to experience for yourself. Add in the cool climate of the city and you are in for an exhilarating experience.

Abha lies about 2,200 meters above sea level and is home to some of the highest peaks in Saudi Arabia.


Due to its mild climate, it is an increasingly popular destination for both locals and international tourists.

Many international options are offered to tourists who want to enjoy adventure, sightseeing and all the wonderful places to visit in Abha.

Al Soudah

Al Soudah is located at an altitude of 3000 meters above sea level, on the side of the highest mountain in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It is covered with juniper mountains and covered with fog throughout the year. Its climate is cold in summer and snow falls on it in winter. It includes the National Park, which is a wonderful tourist destination for camping and hiking And mountain climbing, and Al-Soudah has become a destination for many activities and sporting events during the summer.
