The Eastern Region Of Saudi Arabia: The City Of AlAhisa

You can fall in love with Al-Ahsa from the first visit, as it received a warm reception from the residents, with their distinctive accent and originality, which appears in the mastery of wonderful products, as the people of Al-Ahsa inherited handcrafts, which came according to a natural environment, characterized by fertile land that earned its bounties, to be one of the first cities brimming with industries. Al-Ahsa has enjoyed an important commercial position since the year 960 AD, as it was a gateway to communication with the countries of the world, and a home for the convergence of various civilizations through the port of Al-Uqair, which is the most important historical destination in it. Its land also reveals many archaeological sites, including the Al-Ahsa Oasis, which was included in the UNESCO list. World Heritage. When you visit Al-Ahsa, you will discover fascinating secrets, as its vast desert includes the Ghawar field, the largest oil field in the world, while its farms show you millions of palm trees, as it was classified as the largest palm oases in the world in the Guinness Book of Records, in addition to its richness in eyes, springs and wells.
