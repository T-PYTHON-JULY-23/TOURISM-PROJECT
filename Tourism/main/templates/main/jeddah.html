Jeddah City

The city of Jeddah, immersed in warmth throughout the year, is a captivating spot that welcomes you to be one of the millions who visited it throughout history from all over the world for exploration and trade. Jeddah is the cradle of arts and music that roamed the world, and a global shrine for diverse and vibrant cultures. This mixture left its mark on Jeddah's cuisine, which is distinguished by unique dishes that share the method of preparation with international tastes. When you visit Jeddah, you will have the opportunity to witness a world human heritage celebrated by UNESCO, starting with “Historic Jeddah”, and going through the discovery of the designs of balconies, stone paths and ancient sidewalks, as this wonderful destination holds secrets that inspired artists and writers. Here you will be able to see the most beautiful scene that you can see of the colors of the sunset over the dreamy shores of the Red Sea, while under the water it will continue to give you the charm of the diving experience to see the world’s unique coral reefs that attracted sea voyages to discover their beauty, thus giving the lively city to its visitors endless experiences.

Hotels

Hilton

This 5-star "Hilton" hotel overlooks Jeddah's Corniche and the Red Sea. It offers 5 dining options, 11 flexible meeting rooms and a health club with two swimming pools. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.

The Venue hotel

The Venue Jeddah Corniche is a 5-star hotel located in the heart of Jeddah, overlooking the beautiful Red Sea Riviera waterfront, and only 15 minutes from the world's tallest fountain, "King Fahd's Fountain".

Four Points by Sheraton Jeddah Corniche

At the hotel, the rooms are equipped with a desk. The units at Four Points by Sheraton Jeddah Corniche are air-conditioned, with a private bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries, and a flat-screen TV. Some rooms include a balcony. All rooms in the accommodation will provide you with bed linen and towels.
