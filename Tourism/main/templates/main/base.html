Popular Destination

Makkah

Makkah is one of the world's leading religious tourism destinations and a holy city for Muslims. Located in Saudi Arabia and is the main pilgrimage destination with millions of pilgrims from all over the world flocking to Mecca annually.

Riyadh

Riyadh is the capital of Saudi Arabia and its largest city in terms of population and area. Located in the center of the country, it is a modern and thriving urban center with cultural, economic and commercial diversity.

AlUla

AlUla is a historical and tourist city located in the Medina region of Saudi Arabia. AlUla is an important site on the ancient pilgrimage route and its history dates back thousands of years.

Abha

Abha City is a beautiful city located in the southwest of Saudi Arabia in the Asir region. Abha is the capital of the Asir region and is considered one of the most important tourist cities in the Kingdom.
