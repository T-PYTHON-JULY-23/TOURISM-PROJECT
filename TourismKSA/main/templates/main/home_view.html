The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia has a rich heritage and ancient traditions, as it was a major trading center throughout history and witnessed the birth of the Islamic religion.

The primary language used in public interactions and transactions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is Arabic, which is also its official language. The majority of people in the Kingdom speak English, which is an unofficial second language. Information is shown on all traffic signs in Arabic and English.

The official currency of Saudi Arabia is the Saudi riyal (SAR),and the riyal consists of 100 halalas. Banknotes are available in denominations of 5 riyals, 10 riyals, 50 riyals, 100 riyals, and 500 riyals, and coins in denominations of 1 riyal, 2 riyals, 50 halalas, 25 halalas, 10 halalas, 5 halalas, and 1 halala.
